How It Works

Tournament Format

🏏

Hard Ball Cricket

Full proper hard ball T20 format. Real cricket experience — proper stumps, leather ball, and professional umpiring for every match.

📅

T20 Format

Each match consists of 20 overs per side. Maximum 4 overs per bowler. Power play rules enforced in first 6 overs just like real PSL.

📊

Group Stage

8 teams split into 2 groups of 4. Each team plays 3 group stage matches. Top 2 from each group advance to playoffs.

🏆

Knockouts & Final

Qualifier 1, Eliminator, Qualifier 2, then the Grand Final. Full PSL-style playoff format for maximum drama and excitement.

🎂

Age Eligibility

Players must be between 12 and 16 years old. Age proof (B-Form / CNIC / School ID) required at registration. No exceptions.

👥

Squad Size

Each team registers 15 players + 2 substitutes. Minimum 11 players required to play. Team captain must be 14+ years old.

Fair Play

Tournament Rules

01

Age Verification

All players must present valid age proof (B-Form, School ID, or CNIC) before the tournament. Players found to be over age will disqualify the entire team.

02

Hard Ball Only

All matches will be played with official hard leather cricket ball. Tape ball or tennis ball strictly not allowed. Proper cricket gear mandatory.

03

Safety Equipment

Batting team must wear helmet, gloves, and pads at all times while batting. Wicket keepers must wear full keeping gear. Non-compliance = dismissal.

04

Umpire's Decision Final

All umpire decisions are final and binding. Arguing with umpires results in a 5-run penalty. Second offense results in player suspension for next match.

05

Fair Play Code

Zero tolerance for unsportsmanlike behavior. Players must respect opponents, umpires, and spectators. Any misconduct leads to immediate suspension.

06

Squad Registration

Final squad of 15 players must be submitted 48 hours before tournament start. No changes allowed after submission. All players must be pre-registered.
